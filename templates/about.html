About Voicify by Soham

Learn about our mission and technology

Our Story

Voicify by Soham was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make high-quality text-to-speech technology accessible to everyone. We believe that voice technology should be natural, expressive, and easy to use.

Myself Soham, roleplayed as AI researcher and engineer to worked tirelessly to develop a platform that delivers incredibly natural-sounding speech. By leveraging the latest advancements in deep learning and neural networks, we've created voices that are almost indistinguishable from human speech.

Our Technology

Voicify by Soham uses state-of-the-art AI models powered by ElevenLabs to convert text into natural-sounding speech. Our technology can understand context, emphasize the right words, and even convey emotions through speech.

We're constantly improving our models and adding new features to provide the best possible experience for our users. Whether you're creating content for accessibility, developing applications, or just exploring the possibilities of voice technology, Voicify by Soham is here to help.

Our Values

  • Innovation: We're always pushing the boundaries of what's possible with voice technology.
  • Accessibility: We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality text-to-speech tools.
  • Quality: We're committed to delivering the most natural and expressive voices available.
  • Privacy: We respect your data and privacy, and we're transparent about how we use your information.
