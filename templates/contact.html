Frequently Asked Questions

How does the text-to-speech technology work?

Our text-to-speech technology uses advanced AI models to analyze text and generate natural-sounding speech. The system understands context, emphasis, and can even convey emotions through the generated audio.

What file formats are supported for download?

Currently, we support MP3 format for all generated audio. This ensures compatibility with virtually all devices and platforms.

Is there a limit to how much text I can convert?

The free version allows up to 500 characters per conversion. Premium plans offer higher limits and additional features.

Can I use the generated audio for commercial purposes?

Yes, with our premium plans, you can use the generated audio for commercial purposes, including podcasts, videos, and applications.
